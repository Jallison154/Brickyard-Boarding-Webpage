Our Story

A lifetime of love for dogs

Brickyard Boarding Kennel was founded in 1995, and since then, we've made many friends while caring for countless beloved pets.

Dogs have been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. In grade school, I played "kennel" with our family poodle and mixed breed rather than dolls. My passion for dogs led me to follow in my older sister's footsteps when she started showing dogs at AKC shows in 1975.

My first show dog was a Siberian Husky that became a Champion in 1980 and produced Champion offspring. With over 30 years of experience in dog care, showing, and breeding, you can trust that your pet will receive the highest level of care and attention.
