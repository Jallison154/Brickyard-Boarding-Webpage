Our Story

A lifetime of love for dogs

Brickyard Boarding Kennel was founded in 1995 - we have made many friends. I get to take care of many different breeds.

Dogs have been a part of my life for a very long time. In grade school I played "kennel" with the family poodle and mixed breed rather than Barbies. In 1975 my older sister started showing dogs at the AKC shows so I followed suite.

My first show dog was a Siberian Husky that became a Champion in 1980 and produced Ch. offspring.

With decades of experience in dog care, showing, and breeding, you can trust that your pet will receive the highest level of care and attention at Brickyard Boarding Kennel.

Boarding Agreement

Before bringing your pet to Brickyard Boarding Kennel, please review our boarding agreement:

  • All pets must be current on vaccinations (Rabies, DHPP, Bordetella)
  • Proof of vaccination must be provided at check-in
  • Pets must be in good health and free from contagious conditions
  • Any special dietary needs or medications must be discussed in advance
  • Emergency contact information must be provided and kept current
  • Payment is due at time of service
  • We reserve the right to refuse service to any pet

Note: Please call ahead to schedule your pet's stay and discuss any special requirements.
